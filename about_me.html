About Me

I am Parth Gupta, a seventeen year old boy, living in Nagpur City in Maharashta, India. I grew up in Nagpur and have studied in Modern School, Civil Lines Branch, Modern School, Koradi Road Branch and Centre Point School, Katol Road.

For a change, I started writing. Writing about anything and everything that I can think of. Mostly I will be exploring my perspective towards my life and the problems faced by an average Indian teenager.

I honestly don’t have anything more to write here, but I will soon have a lot to write! That’s my optimism!
