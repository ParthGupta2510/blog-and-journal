Welcome to My Blog & Journal


My first attempt to write a Journal. To many of you, the word Journal might give the idea that it contains all about my personal life. I won’t say that you are wrong but in this Blog/Journal of mine, I will present to you all the amazing experiences that I gain, as well as my perspective towards life.

One’s own mistakes still remain the best teacher. But as said by Eleanor Roosevelt, “Learn from the mistakes of others. You can’t live long enough to make them all yourself.” This is one reason as to why you should consider investing your time in reading my blogs. Another one being, who does not like to laugh at others’ stupidity (lol!)

I hope you enjoy my work and I will be very happy if my work helps you, even in any tiniest way.

I will be posting every Sunday at 8:00 AM IST (UTC+05:30 hours)

Thanks!

- Parth Gupta
